About me

Ayan Sinha is a passionate Indian Athlete from Nagpur settled in Pune Maharashtra. he have represented India in different martial arts platform.

Ayan have expertise in martial arts like Kickboxing, MMA, Pankration, Karate, belt- wrestling and mas-Wrestling. Recently, Ayan Represented India in World Kempo sport (Form of MMA) world championship and have bagged a Gold wining World Championship title.

Ayan is also a part of Indian Olympic Contingent and has represented India in 5th Asian Indoor& Martial Arts Games in 2017, held at Ashgabat, Turkmenistan. In Pankration World Championship Ayan have bagged 2 medals in 2017 and 2018 at Russia and Belarus.
